Our Story

A gentle companion for healthier choices

Cafe Sathi was founded on a simple belief — that food should heal, comfort, and bring people together. Created as a calm escape from busy modern life, the café offers herbal teas, fresh vegetarian dishes, and light meals that preserve natural goodness and promote mindful living.

The name “Sathi,” meaning companion, reflects our purpose: to be a warm, welcoming space where conversations flow, moments slow down, and every sip brings comfort.
